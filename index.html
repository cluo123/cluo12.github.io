As a Ph.D. candidate in the Civil and Systems Engineering Department of Johns Hopkins University, I am under the supervision of Professor James K. Guest in the JHU Topology Optimization Group. My research focus is on developing optimization algorithms to design light-weight and high-performance structures and materials with various manufacturing requirements. Advanced finite element models and efficient topology optimization frameworks are developed to improve performance of materials, components, and structures for many engineering applications.
