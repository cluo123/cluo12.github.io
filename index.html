Chuan Luo

I am a PhD student in the Civil and Systems Engineering Department at Johns Hopkins University, under the supervision of Prof. James K. Guest in the JHU Topology Optimization Group. My research focus on developing optimization algorithms to design light-weight and high-performance structures and materials with various manufacturing requirements. Topology optimization frameworks and advanced finite element models are implemented.

My research interests are in topology optimization for additive manufacturing, multi-physics problems, material nonlinear topology optimization, mechanics and design of composite materials, multi-material topology optimization, and design and optimization of lattice structures. I am currently looking for a postdoctoral position where I can further develop my professional skills and value my research passion.
